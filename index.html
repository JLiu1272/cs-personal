SMIF - Smart Meals, Intelligent Fridge

Teammates: Harshvardhan Gupta, Kyaw Khant Nyar, Robert Aguilera, Christine Van, Candy Wang

1st Prototype has been completed.

Created an IOT device that will address 2 problems (Wasting food and Eating Unhealthy). We called it SMIF. It stands for Smart Meals, Intelligent Fridge.

It does several things:

  1. Keeps track of what you eat
  2. It gives you information about the food in your fridge (i.e Calories/Serving, Fat)
  3. It suggests recipe based on what is available on your fridge
  4. It notifies you when food when food is about to go bad.

More details can be found: SMIF Drive Folder

Laser Puzzle

Laser Puzzle Github

Teammates: Jordan Shae, Moses Lagoon

The puzzle has several rules:

  • Laser
  • Beam
  • Pillar
  • Sockets
  1. Once a laser has been placed, it will shoot its beam in the north and south direction
  2. A pillar blocks the beams from penetrating beyond that point (pillars are represented by the gray box)
  3. Only a certain number of laser can be placed around the sockets (number of lasers are specified by the number of the cell)
  4. You are not allowed to leave any of cells blank (aka, no cells can be white)
Our game will give you hints for possible next moves. If you really become stuck, you can click the solve button, and our algorithm will help you solve it. We are using the concept of backtracking algorithm to solve the puzzle.


Configuration Environment:
- java
- intellij
